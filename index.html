Welcome to Code For Kids!



Code For All

Since the invention of computers, they have come to dominate nearly every aspect of our lives. Code has come to power our phones, cars, music, and entertainment. In the modern world, a knowledge of programming unlocks a whole world of possibilities and opportunities. At Code For Kids, we believe that it is important to learn these skills early. We offer courses in many different programing languages over a wide range of ages, allowing your child to learn vital skills and gain a deep understanding of how stuff works. The courses provide opportunities to learn criticial thinking, teamwork, and social skills that can be applied anywhere. It is our hope that your child will learn new things and have a fun, memorable experience!



About Our Classes

Our classes focus on creating a deep and longlasting understanding of the subject material. We Believe in learning by doing, and allow students many opportunities to use their skills creatively in a hands on fashion. Courses are offered in person or online, and put students in a medium sized class of similar aged students. The classes are ungraded, and student preformance is instead assessed in the form of projects. Course length can vary, but frequently they are between 1-3 weeks.
